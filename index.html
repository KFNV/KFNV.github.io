RAPID TIME

How often do you think about how fleeting time is? We often forget this when we lose ourselves in the routine of the days. We are rushing through time and space into our future, and the days are changing one by one. One way to get out of this chase is to change the situation. Have you noticed how long it takes when you work the first week in a new place? Everything new and unknown causes time to slow down, as we need more time to realize something unusual. That is why one of the best ways to slow down the passage of time is to visit new places, countries and get new emotions.
